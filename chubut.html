Season & Info

  • The season runs from November 1 to April 30
  • You can fish Rainbow, Brown and Brook trout
  • Key rivers: Futaleufú, Rivadavia, Carrileufú, Arrayanes and Corcovado
  • Key lakes: Futalaufquen, Rivadavia, Verde and Cholila
  • World-class water inside Los Alerces National Park
  • Trophy Brown trout and dry fly fishing in pristine surroundings
  • All fishing is catch & release
