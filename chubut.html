Season & Info

  • The season runs from mid-November to April
  • You can fish Rainbow, Brown and Brook trout
  • Key rivers: Carrileufú, Rivadavia, Corcovado and Futaleufu
  • Los Alerces National Park — UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Remote and uncrowded — far fewer anglers than northern Patagonia
  • The valley where Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid once lived
  • All fishing is catch & release

La Pilarica Lodge

Lodge · Carrileufú River · Cholila · Chubut

A five-room lodge on the Carrileufú River, near Los Alerces National Park. Run by two brothers and a cousin, La Pilarica offers all-inclusive fly fishing weeks from November to April, plus horseback riding through one of Patagonia's quietest corners.
