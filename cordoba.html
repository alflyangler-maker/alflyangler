Córdoba

Sierras de Córdoba · Closest trout destination to Buenos Aires

Season & Info

  • The season runs from October to May
  • You can fish Rainbow and Brown trout
  • Key rivers: Los Reartes, San Marcos and Mina Clavero
  • Wild trout in crystal-clear Sierra streams
  • Ideal for beginners and intermediate anglers
  • Closest trout destination to Buenos Aires
  • All fishing is catch & release
