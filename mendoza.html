Season & Info

  • The season runs from November 1 to April 30
  • You can fish Rainbow and Brown trout
  • Key rivers: Atuel, Diamante, Grande, Malargüe and Tunuyán
  • Key waters: Laguna del Diamante and the high-Andes lagoons
  • High-altitude desert fishing with dramatic mountain scenery
  • Best dry fly fishing: December & January
  • All fishing is catch & release
