Season & Info

  • The season runs from late September to May
  • Target species: Golden Dorado
  • Key fisheries: Iberá Wetlands, Upper and Middle Paraná River
  • Fish from skiffs on crystal-clear channels — ideal for sight fishing with poppers and streamers
  • Dorado average 5 to 20 lbs, with fish pushing 30+ lbs on the Paraná
  • All fishing is catch & release
