Neuquén

Lanín & Nahuel Huapi National Parks · Northern Patagonia

Season & Info

  • The season runs from November 1 to April 30
  • You can fish Rainbow and Brown trout
  • Key rivers: Malleo, Chimehuin, Collon Cura, Alumine and Quillen
  • Key lakes: Huechulafquen, Tromen, Alumine, Paimún and Lolog
  • World-class dry fly hatches on the Malleo River
  • Trophy Browns and Rainbows in the 20"+ range
  • All fishing is catch & release
