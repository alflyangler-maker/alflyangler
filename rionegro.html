Season & Info

  • The season runs from November 1 to April 30
  • You can fish Rainbow, Brown and Brook trout
  • Key rivers: Limay, Manso, Traful and Caleufu
  • Key lakes: Nahuel Huapi, Mascardi, Fonck, Hess and Roca
  • Best dry fly fishing: January & February
  • Best for trophy Brown trout: March to May
  • All fishing is catch & release
