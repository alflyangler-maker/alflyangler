Season & Info

  • The season runs from November to May
  • Target species: Rainbow Trout and Chinook Salmon
  • Key fishery: Lago Strobel (Jurassic Lake) — one of the most remote and untouched in Patagonia
  • Average Rainbow close to 10 lbs, with fish breaking 20 lbs caught almost weekly
  • Also fishes: Barrancoso River and Santa Cruz River
  • All fishing is catch & release
