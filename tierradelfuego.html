Tierra del Fuego

End of the Earth · Río Grande · Sea-run Brown Trout

Season & Info

  • The season runs from December to April
  • Target species: Sea-run Brown trout
  • Key river: Río Grande — home to the world's largest run of sea trout
  • Average fish in the 7 to 15 lb range, with real shots at 20+ lbs
  • Peak run: January to mid-March
  • Recommended technique: Spey rods and sinking lines
  • All fishing is catch & release
